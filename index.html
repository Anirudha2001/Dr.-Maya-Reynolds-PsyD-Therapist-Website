About Dr. Reynolds

A Psychologist Who Truly Listens

I'm Dr. Maya Reynolds, a licensed psychologist (PsyD) based in Santa Monica, California. I specialize in helping adults who are struggling with anxiety, trauma, and the weight of past experiences that follow them into everyday life.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and deeply individualized. I believe that healing isn't one-size-fits-all — which is why I draw from multiple evidence-based modalities including EMDR, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, and mindfulness-based practices, tailoring every session to your unique needs and goals.

I work primarily with adults navigating anxiety disorders, PTSD, depression, life transitions, and relationship challenges. Whether you're coming in for the first time or have worked with a therapist before, I'm here to meet you where you are.

My Therapeutic Approach

Evidence-Based. Warmly Human.

I believe that effective therapy combines the rigor of science with the art of genuine human connection. No two people are alike, and neither should their treatment be. I draw from a range of proven approaches to create a plan that's truly yours.

Sessions are collaborative — you set the pace. Whether we're working through the roots of anxiety, processing trauma, or building skills for daily resilience, I'm with you every step of the way with compassion, honesty, and clinical expertise.
